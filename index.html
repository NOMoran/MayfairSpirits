Location& Hours

Visit us at Mayfair Wine and Spirits, conveniently located in Wauwatosa. We're open 7 days a week from 9am to 9pm to serve all your wine, spirits, and beverage needs.

Our address: 739 N Mayfair Road, Wauwatosa, WI 53226
Phone: (414) 249-4584

Mission

At Mayfair Spirits, our mission is to provide our community with a curated selection of quality wines, spirits, and craft beers, delivered with exceptional service and expert guidance. We strive to create a welcoming space where every customer can discover the perfect drink for any occasion.

About

Mayfair Spirits Wine & More

Located at 739 N Mayfair Rd, Wauwatosa, WI 53226, Mayfair Spirits Wine & More is your one-stop destination for premium beverages. We offer a curated selection of fine wines, top-shelf spirits, and craft beers, catering to both connoisseurs and casual drinkers alike. Whether you're searching for the perfect bottle for a celebration, a unique gift, or simply stocking up for your personal collection, our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you. Stop by and experience exceptional service and a wide variety of quality products at Mayfair Spirits Wine & More.
